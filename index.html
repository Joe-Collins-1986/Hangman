Hangman

Rules

Welcome to the game of Hangman. These rules are very simple.


  • The game will present you with a hidden word in the form of underscores (one underscore for each letter in the word).
  • Guess the letters you believe may be in the word.
  • If you guess right the letter will appear, if you are wrong part of the hangman will be drawn.
  • There are 10 parts to the hangman, therefore you are allowed up to 9 incorrect letters when guessing the word.
  • If you guess the word before the hangman is complete you win and be awarded points. The number of points awarded is dependent on how many letters you get wrong. If you guess the word without any mistakes you get 10 points, each incorrect answer reduces your score by 1 point.
  • If you fail to guess the word before the hangman is complete you lose and will have 5 points deducted from your overall score.
  • You can change the category by going to settings and selecting a different option.
  • Now the most important rule. Have fun!!!
